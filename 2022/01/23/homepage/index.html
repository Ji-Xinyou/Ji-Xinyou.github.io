This is my first post to this blog website! 😋
It is really exciting for me to have the chance to really say something on the Internet.
There are mainly 3️⃣ reasons for me to start the blog.

  • ⭐ Reading others’ blog really inspires me

    • Others’ sharing experiences on blogs really helps me through many mind-windling 😱 situations, and I really want to be one of them. Hopefully my experience will help the readers as well 😄

  • ☝️ Learning without recording is not as effective

    • This came across to my mind when I am taking 6.S081: Operating System Engineering (great course btw). There are so many important details and great minds inside an O/S, which I think should be well-learned and recorded.

  • 💥 SHARING IS ALWAYS FUN!

    • The thrill of sharing takes up the most part of why I am starting this blog! Not only sharing the experience is rewarding 😋, but sharing my daily life is also exciting.

To everyone read this page:

May the force be with you
